Bio

Hey there! I'm Thomas, a budding programmer who's always eager to learn and create. Although I've only been learning IT for a few years, I'm constantly amazed by the endless possibilities it offers. Whether I'm working on a new project or experimenting with new technologies, I'm always curious to see what's possible. One of the things I love most about IT is the ability to take an idea and turn it into something real. I'm a firm believer that anything is possible with the right mindset and hard work. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me exploring new hobbies or brainstorming new ideas.

Thanks for taking the time to check out my portfolio – I can't wait to see what the future holds!

My works

In the next sections, you can witness my journey over the past two years and see how my passion for programming has grown and evolved through my works.

C/C++ fundamentals View details

Diving into a new area of learning as well as into a new (programming) language is for me a stimulating passage but at the same time a dizzying jump in the middle of a sea of knowledge.
In these cases, I like to take the time to move forward cautiously by circling the objective as broadly as possible for fear of losing essential notions for the next step.
Then, when I have gained confidence, I love that moment when it clicks into myself and when I feel fully engaged in the experimentation process.
