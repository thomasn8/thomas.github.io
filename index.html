Develop a 3d game in C based on raycasting principles

Summary

Inspired by early FPS games, the goal of this project was to build a dynamic view inside a maze. Based on trigonometric rules I was able to convert distances of casted rays into rows of pixels on the screen.

Insights

Still quite new in the field of computing, I was really surprised by the speed of calculations of the processor to retranscribe instantly the movement events in pixel.

~ 3 weeks
